Stone Paper Scissors

Rules of Stone-Paper-Scissors Stone-Paper-Scissors is a simple game played between two people. Each player has to choose one of three shapes: Stone Paper Scissors Winning Rules The winner is determined by the following rules: 1. Stone beats Scissors (Stone crushes Scissors) 2. Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper) 3. Paper beats Stone (Paper covers Stone) Draw Rule If both players choose the same shape, the game is a draw and must be replayed. How to Play 1. The winner is determined based on the rules above. 2. If it's a draw, the players go again until there is a winner.
